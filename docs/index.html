About This Project

Our Mission

Creating a curated repository of online AI courses to help people on their AI journey. We believe that AI education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or financial situation.

What We Offer

A comprehensive catalog of AI training courses from leading platforms like UiPath Academy, AWS Skill Builder, Google Cloud Skillsboost, Microsoft Learn, Coursera, DeepLearning.AI, and many more.

How It Works

Browse, search, and filter courses by provider, skill level, difficulty, and learning track. Click on any course to visit the provider's website and start learning immediately.
